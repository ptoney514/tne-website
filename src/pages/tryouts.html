Frequently Asked Questions

What if my child can't make the scheduled tryout date?
We understand schedules can be tricky. If your child cannot attend the scheduled tryout, please contact us at tryouts@tneexpress.com to discuss alternative arrangements. We try to accommodate all serious players.
How are teams selected?
Players are evaluated on skill level, athleticism, coachability, and attitude. Our coaches look for players who show dedication, hustle, and a willingness to learn. Team selections are typically announced within 48 hours of the tryout.
What is the practice and game schedule like?
Teams typically practice 2-3 times per week (evenings) with games on Saturdays. Tournament schedules vary but usually include 3-5 tournaments per season. Specific schedules are provided once teams are formed.
Is there financial assistance available?
Yes! We believe every dedicated player should have the opportunity to play. We offer payment plans and limited scholarship assistance based on need. Contact us to discuss your situation - we'll work with you to find a solution.
What does the season fee cover?
Season fees cover uniforms (jersey, shorts), practice facility rentals, coaching staff, league registration, 3-5 tournament entry fees, and team equipment. Additional tournaments may require extra fees.
